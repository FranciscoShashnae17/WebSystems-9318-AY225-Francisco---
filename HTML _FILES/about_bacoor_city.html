History

Bacoor City, located in Cavite, Philippines, is a historic and vibrant community with a rich cultural heritage. Known for its pivotal role in the Philippine Revolution and strong community values, Bacoor has grown into a progressive and inclusive city.

Vision

A safe, progressive, and inclusive city that empowers its citizens and promotes sustainable growth.

Mission

To provide efficient, transparent, and citizen-centered services through good governance, innovation, and community engagement.

Core Values

  • Integrity – Upholding honesty and accountability in all actions.
  • Service Excellence – Delivering high-quality services to our residents.
  • Community Engagement – Encouraging active participation from citizens.
  • Innovation – Implementing modern solutions for urban development.
  • Inclusivity – Ensuring all members of the community are valued and respected.
